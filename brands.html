Brands

About GILGAN

Gilgan Company Ltd. is the umbrella company for popular brands Bamboo Shack, Bamboo Grillhouse and Frankie Gone Bananas. GilganJam International is the Corporate Franchise located in South Florida.  Our goal is to provide first class service and amazing experience for each of our customers daily. Each brand is uniquely different but represents the Gilgan core values:  • Cleanliness  • Consistency  • Delicious Quality food  • Exceptional service  • Excellence 

About Bamboo Shack

Since opening a small shack on Nassau Street in 1990, Bamboo Shack remains committed to community and being deliciously Bahamian; over the years, the brand has become synonymous with Bahamian culture. 

It all began with the dream of a young girl who came from humble beginnings who wanted to make the best tasting chicken in the world. It began with 1 location, 2 employees and a strong commitment to excellence; since then, Elaine Pinder’s dreams are coming to fruition with with 7 locations including one in South Florida. 

Read more at www.bambooshack242.com

About Bamboo Grillhouse

The newest addition to the Gilgan Family is Bamboo Grillhouse located in the West Bay (Hoffer’s) Shopping Plaza on West Bay Street. 

In addition to the popular Bamboo Classics - thigh, conch, cracked chicken and jerk snacks - Bamboo Grillhouse offers a variety of fresh salads, rice bowls and grilled options. 

Satisfy all of your cravings at Bamboo Grill House...after all, it’s “Delicious Done Right!” 

Learn more at www.bambooshack242.com

About Frankie Gone Bananas at Bimini Road

On the easternmost end of the world famous Arawak Cay “Fish Fry” sits Frankie Gone Bananas “Frankie’s”, an authentically Bahamian restaurant known for its tasty seafood and native dishes. 

Bahamians and tourists alike frequent the restaurant for its food, flavor, fun and family oriented atmosphere. Frankie’s courtyard, Conch Salad Bar, Martini Lounge, Sunset Deck overlooking the scenic Nassau Harbour and Ole Man Frankie’s Shot Bar are just a few reasons why people can’t get enough of Frankie’s! 

A cultural hub dedicated to all things Bahamian, watch Frankie’s come alive with live entertainment by Bahamian artists and musicians. 

To learn more, visit www.frankiegbananas.com
